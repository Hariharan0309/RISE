# RISE – 3-Minute Demo Script & Recording Guide

## Full script (~3 minutes when spoken at a steady pace)

**[0:00 – 0:20] Intro**  
“This is RISE — Rural Innovation and Sustainable Ecosystem: an AI-powered farming assistant for Indian farmers. It runs in the browser, works in multiple Indian languages, and helps with crop disease, weather, schemes, markets, and more. I’ll show you the main features in about three minutes.”

**[0:20 – 0:45] Login & home**  
“First, I log in with name, phone, and location. The sidebar lists all features. The home screen is Chat and Diagnosis: I can type questions or upload a crop photo. I’ll open Crop Disease, upload an image, add crop type and symptoms, and run analysis. The app uses AI to detect diseases and suggest treatments, and it can store the result in the cloud.”

**[0:45 – 1:10] Schemes & market**  
“Under Scheme Discovery, farmers can browse government schemes like PM-KISAN and PMFBY, see eligibility and benefits, and get AI-summarized recommendations. Scheme Application helps with applying. Then Market Prices: I pick a state and commodity to see current prices and AI insights on trends.”

**[1:10 – 1:35] Equipment, buying groups, suppliers**  
“Equipment Booking and Equipment Marketplace are for renting or buying tractors and machinery. Buying Groups lets farmers form groups to get better bulk prices. Supplier Negotiation helps them request quotes from multiple suppliers and compare. Buyer Connection connects farmers to buyers with price and quality preferences.”

**[1:35 – 2:00] Calculators & alerts**  
“Profitability Calculator estimates returns for a crop and land size. Loan Calculator helps assess financing needs and shows an AI summary. Alert Customization sets what notifications they get. Local Economy shows nearby mandis and services. Best Practices gives proven farming tips and lets users adopt and share practices.”

**[2:00 – 2:25] Weather & voice**  
“Weather Alerts shows forecasts and AI-generated advice. Voice Input lets farmers ask questions by speaking instead of typing — useful in the field. The app supports Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, and more from the language selector.”

**[2:25 – 2:50] Offline & wrap-up**  
“RISE can work offline: key data is cached, and when back online it syncs. Status in the sidebar shows if the AI agent and AWS are connected. User profile is at the bottom; logout is there too.”

**[2:50 – 3:00] Closing**  
“That’s RISE: one place for diagnosis, schemes, markets, equipment, buying groups, suppliers, loans, weather, and voice — built with Strands Agents and AWS Bedrock for Indian farmers. Thanks for watching.”

## How to record the video

### Option 1: OBS Studio (free, full control)

1. **Install:** [obsproject.com](https://obsproject.com) — choose your OS.
2. **Scene setup**
   - Add **Display Capture** (full screen) or **Window Capture** (only the browser).
   - Add **Audio Input Capture** (microphone) so your voice is recorded.
3. **Settings**
   - **Settings → Output**: Recording format MP4 or MKV; encoder (e.g. x264 or hardware encoder).
   - **Settings → Audio**: Sample rate 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz; channels Stereo.
4. **Record**
   - Click **Start Recording**, run the app and speak the script, then **Stop Recording**.
   - File is in **Videos** (or the folder you set in OBS).

### Option 2: Browser extension (simple)

- **Chrome:** “Screen Recorder” or “Loom” (or similar).
- **Firefox:** “Awesome Screenshot” or “Nimbus Screenshot” (screen + mic).
- Open RISE in the browser, start the extension’s “Record tab” or “Record screen + mic”, then follow the script. Stop and download the video.

### Option 3: Built-in OS recorder

- **Windows:** `Win + G` → “Capture” → record (include mic in Xbox Game Bar settings).
- **macOS:** QuickTime Player → File → New Screen Recording → choose mic for narration.
- **Linux:** GNOME (e.g. Ubuntu): open “Settings → Keyboard → View and customize shortcuts → Screenshots” and enable “Record a short video of the screen”, or use **SimpleScreenRecorder** / **OBS**.

### Tips for a good 3-minute demo

1. **Prepare**
   - Log in once; have a crop image ready for disease analysis.
   - Resize browser so UI and text are readable (e.g. 1280×720 or 1920×1080).
   - Close extra tabs and mute other apps to avoid notifications.

2. **Pace**
   - Speak the script at a steady pace; 3 minutes ≈ 400–450 words.
   - Pause briefly when switching features so viewers can see the screen.

3. **Audio**
   - Use a headset or quiet room so the voice is clear.
   - Do a short test record (e.g. 20 seconds) and check volume before the full take.

4. **What to show**
   - Follow the script order: Login → Chat/Diagnosis (and one crop analysis) → Scheme Discovery → Market Prices → one of Equipment/Buying Groups/Supplier → Profitability or Loan → Weather → Voice → sidebar (language, offline, profile) → end.

5. **Export**
   - Keep format as MP4 (or convert to MP4) for easy upload to YouTube, Google Drive, or the hackathon portal.
